How it works.

See where you rank

Compare your resume with other applicants. How does your experience level fit in with your competition? Understand your pitfalls as well as where your resume shines. Our powerful computer models give you the necessary feedback to make your resume a success.

Got hired? Negotiate your salary

We’ll pay you to make your resume visible to the rest of the applicant pool who didn’t get hired. Don’t worry, we blur any personally identifiable information.

If you make the final cut, we prefer you list your salary. We then immediately list the salaries of all candidates who were turned down for the same position, but hired elsewhere. This way you’re informed of any discrepancies and have leverage to negotiate a mightier salary.

Didn’t get the job?

Not all’s lost. We’ll let you view information about the people who ended up getting hired, so you know exactly what your resume could have missed and how to improve your prospects for similar jobs next time you apply.
